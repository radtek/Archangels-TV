User Privileges Summary

SUMMARY

Saturday, October 10, 2015
Version 1.0

SatView™ protects most of its services by implementing an account based security mechanism where each user is represented by such an account describing his rights in form of a privilege profile. User accounts may be limited in time, a suitable feature for guest users. In addition, predefined privilege profiles simplify the account management.
