Databases within SatView™ contain many different components, like tables, displays or procedures, most of them interlinked with each other. Depending on the nature of those components, various customizable user interfaces are available that support the database maintenance in a comprehensive way.

Table Forms

Table Forms are the interface available to create, modify, delete or simply browse database component items hosted by the (relational) DBMS. They present the information within tables that are directly modifiable. In addition, the tables are arranged in a way showing the relationship between them which significantly simplifies the interpretation of the data.

Mimics Displays

In the context of designing Mimics Displays there is an easy-to-use interface consisting of two panes: The upper one allowing the interactive arrangement of the mimics objects and the lower one hosting the coding area. Special features are also available to test the design before using it in the operational environment.

Automation Scripts

The development of Automation Scripts happens in an integrated way as well: Editing, compiling and testing an automation script takes place within the same interface. It is divided into two panes: The upper one contains a coding area and the lower one renders the output of the tested script.

Telecommand Procedures

The design of Telecommand Procedures involves two parts as there are the definition of the execution paths or program flow (which is usually done in an interactive graphical way) and the specification of additional properties necessary to generate the executable code. These two parts are reflected by the two panes of the interface: The left one hosting the graphical area and the right one properties area.
